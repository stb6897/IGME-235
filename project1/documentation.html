Design Process

Portfolio Goal

My goal with this portfolio is to show the projects that I am most proud of so that employers can see my experience.

Audience

The audience for my portfolio is anyone who is interested in my professional career. So this could be employers, family, mentors, etc.

Content I Want To Include

I would like to include projects that involved both my work done at school and my experiences outside of school. For example this could be games or mods that represent my experience as a programmer and game developer. I've also included my resume and contact information if anyone wants to reach out to me.

How Is My Content Organized?

I have a nice header at the top that includes my name, tagline, and a navigation bar. This is then followed by a picture of me and an about section. I then have a nice list of projects with an image on the left and text on the right. Lastly there is a footer at the bottom with contact info.
